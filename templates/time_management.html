Synopsis

One of the main issues that student face in school is with time management. Many college students find it difficult to find a balance between school, social life, hobbies, and possibly a job. During college years, it is a time period where many students are in a point in their life where they are discovering themselves and being molded into the person they will become in the future. Having to jugle between the aspects of life mentioned above, and can have affect on students' performance in school.


Affects on students

Research has shown that proper time management is highly related to academic success in college students. According to Muhammaed Saqib Khan, a professor at Gomal University in Pakistan, there is a significant and positive relation between time planning, time management, and academic performance in students. Time planning is an essential part of being in college and should be practiced as early as possible to ensure students have a higher possibilty of succeeding.
